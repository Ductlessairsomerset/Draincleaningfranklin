**Plumbing Prep: Protect Pipes and Water Heaters**
Cold weather puts major stress on your plumbing. As the ground freezes, pipes are at risk of bursting, which is easily one of the most destructive home repairs.

Start by insulating exposed pipes. Check pipes in unheated areas like basements, crawl spaces, and garages. Add insulation sleeves to any exposed pipes to prevent freezing.

Next, drain your outdoor faucets. Disconnect garden hoses and drain water from spigots to prevent ice damage. You may need a Plumber Franklin Township to properly winterize your exterior lines.
